OUR MISSION

WHO WE ARE

A brewery is 99% created out of passion for beer and is often one hobby that got out of hand. As a brewer you prefer to be busy keep brewing beer! This is no different at Kwartje brewery. After a short period as a tenant brewer it was decided to own brewery. This is a successful one in 2016 crowdfunding campaign moving forward. The brewers are next door brewery working in IT. This is reflected in the naming of most beers. The name Kwartje comes from the eponymous group of friends from the past where the brewers meet got to know. Years later it was decided to brew beer to revive this name.
